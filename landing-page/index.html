Ahvoda Recruitment

Ahvoda? Ahvoda.

Job-hunting is a full-time job. But does it need to be? If jobs came to you, then you wouldn't need to go looking at all.

Ahvoda means work; it does the hard work of finding a job for you. When a job posting that matches you is posted, you're directly notified. Applying is then a one-step process.

So stop crawling through mounds of disorganized and irrelevant postings on classifieds. Let Ahvoda work for you, to provide the perfect job at the perfect moment.

How?

Passive search

Both businesses and job-hunters create profiles with their relevant information on Ahvoda. Behind the scenes, we use efficient matching algorithms to evaluate how likely each employee would enjoy each job in order to calculate a MatchScore.

When a job is whose MatchScore is above a certain threshold is found, you receive a notification directly on your phone, or via email, from which you can apply to that job in one click.

Active search

Leverage the power of MatchScore to see jobs you're more likely to enjoy as you scroll through job postings. Again, applying to each job is right at your fingertips.
